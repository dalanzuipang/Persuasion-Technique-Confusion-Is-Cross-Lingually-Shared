    "# 05 -- Rank correlations, subset treatments, Table IV and Figure 3 (RQ2)\n",
    "\n",
    "The central result. Three things are computed here:\n",
    "\n",
    "1. **Cross-lingual correlation** -- the robust part. -0.004 (article) ->\n",
    "   +0.356 (paragraph, all) -> +0.198 (paragraph, intersection). Positive under\n",
    "   every paragraph-level treatment.\n",
    "2. **Paradigm complementarity** -- reverses sign under either treatment.\n",
    "3. **delta_rho** -- **not identified**: +0.076 (p=0.25) over all paragraphs,\n",
    "   +0.365 (p=0.005) under the intersection. A factor of nearly five on the\n",
    "   same predictions and the same technique sub-space.\n",
    "\n",
    "The second free parameter: the TCM sees only units where gold and predictions\n",
    "are both non-empty, so the three matrices of a language rest on different\n",
    "paragraph subsets. Restricting to the paragraphs on which all three detectors\n",
    "fire is the obvious control -- and it is **not neutral**. We report both and\n",
    "claim neither delta_rho value."
